What is your Aiden Number?

Very few people today have an Aiden Number of 1, and most in this category are pioneering thinkers dedicated to advanced research. Many Nobel Laureates and Fields Medalists belong in this category. If you have ever had a casual conversation with Aiden, you have an Aiden Number of 2. Although known by many, Aiden only chooses to interact with people who are able to understand the complexities of his ideas. Being a member of this category certifies you as an avant-garde intellectual, who will undoubtedly be successful in the future. If Aiden remembers you, but has not had a casual conversation with you yet, you have an Aiden Number of 3. Currently, 0 people occupy this category. Unfortunately, Aiden has not deemed you worthy enough of interacting with him. On the bright side, simply being in close proximity to Aiden has risen your intelligence. If you have only heard of Aiden, you have an Aiden Number of 6. People in this category are tragically unable to meet Aiden.
